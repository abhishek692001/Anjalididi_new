Ayurvedic Principles

Ayurveda is based on the belief that health and wellness depend on a delicate balance between the mind, body, and spirit. The primary goal of Ayurvedic medicine is to promote good health, rather than fight disease.

Vata

Composed of Space and Air, Vata governs movement and is responsible for basic body processes such as breathing, cell division and circulation.

  • Promotes creativity and flexibility
  • Controls movement and communication
  • Governs enthusiasm and change

Pitta

Representing Fire and Water, Pitta governs metabolism and transformation in the body, including digestion and absorption of nutrients.

  • Enhances understanding and intelligence
  • Maintains body temperature
  • Processes perceptions and experiences

Kapha

Made up of Earth and Water, Kapha forms the body's structure and provides lubrication to the body parts, maintaining immunity and protecting tissues.

  • Provides physical strength and endurance
  • Supports healing and regeneration
  • Offers stability and emotional calmness

Medicinal Plants

Ayurvedic medicine harnesses the healing power of plants and herbs, using their natural properties to restore balance and promote wellness. At Tatvam Ayurved, we carefully select the highest quality medicinal plants for our treatments and products.

Tulsi

Also known as Holy Basil, Tulsi is considered one of the most sacred herbs in Ayurveda, known for its healing properties.

  • Supports respiratory health
  • Boosts immunity
  • Reduces stress and anxiety

Ashwagandha

A powerful adaptogenic herb that helps the body manage stress and promotes overall wellbeing.

  • Enhances energy and vitality
  • Improves mental clarity
  • Supports hormonal balance

Neem

Renowned for its powerful antimicrobial and purifying properties, Neem has been used in Ayurvedic medicine for thousands of years.

  • Purifies blood
  • Supports skin health
  • Aids in detoxification
