Ayurvedic Principles

Ayurveda is based on the belief that health and wellness depend on a delicate balance between the mind, body, and spirit. The primary goal of Ayurvedic medicine is to promote good health, rather than fight disease.

Vata

Composed of Space and Air

Vata governs movement and is responsible for basic body processes such as breathing, cell division and circulation.

  • Promotes creativity and flexibility
  • Controls movement and communication
  • Governs enthusiasm and change

Pitta

Representing Fire and Water

Pitta governs metabolism and transformation in the body, including digestion and absorption of nutrients.

  • Enhances understanding and intelligence
  • Maintains body temperature
  • Processes perceptions and experiences

Kapha

Made up of Earth and Water

Kapha forms the body's structure and provides lubrication to the body parts, maintaining immunity and protecting tissues.

  • Provides physical strength and endurance
  • Supports healing and regeneration
  • Offers stability and emotional calmness

Adaptogen

Ashwagandha

Known as "Indian Ginseng," Ashwagandha helps reduce stress and anxiety, improves brain function, and boosts immunity.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduces cortisol levels
  • Enhances physical performance
  • Supports adrenal function
Anti-inflammatory

Turmeric (Haldi)

A powerful anti-inflammatory herb containing curcumin, turmeric helps reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and support joint health.

Key Benefits:

  • Powerful antioxidant properties
  • Supports healthy joints
  • Enhances skin health
Brain Tonic

Brahmi (Bacopa)

A renowned brain tonic that enhances memory, concentration, and cognitive function while reducing anxiety and stress.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves memory retention
  • Enhances mental clarity
  • Supports nervous system health
Antioxidant

Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

One of the richest natural sources of Vitamin C, Amla strengthens immunity, improves digestion, and promotes healthy skin and hair.

Key Benefits:

  • Boosts immune function
  • Supports hair growth and health
  • Enhances skin radiance
Adaptogen

Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Revered as "The Queen of Herbs," Tulsi helps combat stress, supports respiratory health, and enhances immunity.

Key Benefits:

  • Supports respiratory health
  • Balances stress hormones
  • Purifies blood and detoxifies
Antimicrobial

Neem

A powerful antimicrobial herb that purifies blood, detoxifies the body, and treats skin conditions while supporting liver health.
